Let's Talk!

In this public talk, speakers from the Extinction Rebellion Boston will share the latest climate science on where our planet is heading, discuss some of the current psychology around climate change, and offer solutions through the study of social movements.

We will be travelling around Massachusetts to give this talk everywhere we can and share what we know about the climate emergency. If you'd like for us to visit your community to give the 'Heading to Extinction (and what to do about it)' talk, fill out the form below. We are happy to talk to audiences small and large.

We may ask you to help us advertise the event on Facebook, with flyers, or through word-of-mouth. If you have a venue to suggest, whether it's your living room or a community center, please include that in the comments below. In our experience, this talk is a good way to start a conversation about the ongoing crisis in a supportive and understanding atmosphere.

We'll be in touch!
