Boston Globe Campaign

On April 17, 2019, Extinction Rebellion Massachusetts demonstrated at the Boston Globe, urging them to tell the truth about the climate and ecological crisis we face.

  • Read and sign on to our call to action, which was also published in Dig Boston
  • Read our press release about the call to action and compare it with the Globe's coverage of the event (they did not send a reporter downstairs to report on our demonstration in their building)
  • Finally, we ask you to demand that the Boston Globe take this issue seriously
