Communication

Each working group may have its own mailing list. Check with the coordinator.

Keybase is a secure, end-to-end encrypted messaging app (for Windows, Mac, iOS, Android) which Extinction Rebellion US chapters have been using to communicate (to each other, and internally).

If you want to sign up, there’s a useful intro here. Send a message to timelessway or savetheclimate19, and we can add you to the Extinction Rebellion Boston / Massachusetts teams for chat.

Shared Files

This public Google Drive has poster templates, fliers, research, meeting notes etc. There are also compilations of links to other XR chapters’ resources. When you make or find a useful resource, please post it here to share! No sensitive action info should be posted.

Public Calendar

If you need events posted on this calendar, send date, time, location and description to your working group coordinator. We will post our own events here (art builds, meetings, talks, actions, etc.), and we will selectively promote events of other groups in line with XR’s mission.
